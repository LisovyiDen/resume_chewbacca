Chewbacca

Chewbacca is the loyal friend and first mate of Han Solo, and serves as co-pilot on Solo's spaceship, the Millennium Falcon; together they help the Rebel Alliance defeat the Galactic Empire and restore freedom to the galaxy.

Smuggler With Han Solo

19 BBY to 4 ABY | Galaxy

  • Around 3 BBY, Chewbacca accompanied Han Solo to the Mondder Spaceport on Etti IV in the Corporate Sector
  • The years went on as Chewbacca accompanied Solo on treasure hunts and spice runs
  • Chewbacca and Han were contacted by Jabba, who wanted the two to locate the priceless Yavin Vassilika.

Rebel Aliance

4 ABY to 20 ABY | Galaxy

  • After the discovery of Princess Leia's presence on the station, Luke convinced Solo and Chewbacca to help him rescue the princess.
  • Chewbacca accompanied the others to the Rebel base of Yavin 4 where they were preparing for their strike at the Death Star.
  • Chewbacca managed to convince Han to return and help the Rebels. Han finally gave in, helping Luke Skywalker destroy the Death Star.

Hero of the Rebel Alliance Hero

20 ABY to 25 ABY | Galaxy

  • Broke and fearing Jabba's retribution, Solo and Chewbacca discovered an ancient cache of treasure hidden in one of Yavin IV's temples. He and Han stole the treasure, against Chewie's wishes.
  • Several months after the Battle of Yavin, the two assisted Renegade Squadron, an elite Rebel fighting force created by Han prior to Yavin, during the Battle of the Graveyard.
  • Shortly before Life Day, Chewbacca returned to duty in the Rebel Alliance.
  • Once the Rebels were spread out across the galaxy, Admiral Ackbar sent Chewbacca and Han to Void Station where they encountered bounty hunter, Sheel Odala.
